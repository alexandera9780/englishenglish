About this Project

This project was meant to show the preservation of coding. To me, coding is pretty much the langauge of the world. All the digital technology and computers and phones you see today are all created through code. This is being threatened by AI, which is slowly taking over the industry of technology. If AI takes over, people will become lazy and will stop learning to code, and eventually, there gets to a point where we won't be able to fix things and everything will appear the same.
